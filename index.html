Service 02

INTERIOR CLEANING

Why Our Products?

SAFE FOR YOUR INTERIOR

At E Mobile Car Wash, we only use pH-balanced, leather-safe interior cleaners that clean deep without degrading your materials. Many cheap products strip natural oils from leather, causing cracking and premature ageing — ours are specifically formulated to protect as they clean.

Our fabric and carpet shampoos penetrate fibres to lift embedded dirt, food and odour-causing bacteria without leaving behind residue that attracts more dirt. The result is a genuinely fresh, clean interior that stays cleaner for longer.

We use non-silicone dashboard dressings — no greasy sheen that reflects into your windscreen. Instead, a natural matte finish that won't cause glare or attract dust buildup.

Most importantly: our products will NOT rot your interior. No harsh solvents, no bleaching agents — just professional-grade, safe-use formulations trusted by detailers worldwide.

Service 04

HEADLIGHT RESTORATION

Before and After

SEE THE DIFFERENCE

Yellowed, oxidised headlight lenses reduce your night visibility by up to 80% — a serious safety concern. Our restoration process removes the UV-damaged outer layer, polishes to full clarity, then seals with a UV protective coating to prevent future yellowing.

The result is crystal-clear lenses, dramatically improved light output and a front-end that looks years younger — all at a fraction of replacement cost.

Our Arsenal

THE PRODUCTS SHELF

Why It Matters

PROFESSIONAL-GRADE CHEMICALS

Not all car cleaning chemicals are equal. We invest in professional-grade detailing products used by the world's best auto detailers — because your car deserves better than generic supermarket cleaners.

Our degreasers and pre-soaks are formulated to dissolve oil, road tar, brake dust and organic contamination at the molecular level — lifting them away safely without scratching or damaging clear-coat or paint.

Interior chemicals are selected for their enzyme-based cleaning action — they break down biological contaminants like food, sweat and pet dander rather than just masking them with fragrance. No residue. Just clean.

We maintain a full shelf of specialist products — separate chemicals for leather, fabric, rubber, glass, chrome and plastic — because one chemical should never be used on everything.

Every product we use is biodegradable and eco-conscious where possible, and none contain formaldehyde, bleach or acid-based solvents that destroy interiors over time.
